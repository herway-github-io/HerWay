Luxury Girl Workshop

Calling all elagant and boujee women! Dive into the art of tent design, where sophistication meets the wilderness.

Luxury Girl Workshop

The 'Luxury Girl Workshop' is a tent design workshop that invites women to explore the elegant side of outdoor living. This workshop is a creative haven where you’ll learn to design your (before) average tent and turn it into a luxurious tent that blends comfort with the beauty of nature. From selecting sumptuous fabrics to incorporating eco-friendly materials, our sessions cover all aspects of creating a lavish retreat. Expert designers will guide you through the process of transforming a simple tent into a sumptuous sanctuary, complete with all the amenities of a boutique hotel. Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast or a design aficionado, “Glamping Glamour” will provide you with the skills to elevate any camping experience into a stylish escape. Join us and discover how to turn the wilderness into a wonderland of comfort and class.

Survivor's Workshop

For women who always seek for new adventures. Who enjoy pushing boundaries and exploring new ways to connect with nature.

Survivor's Workshop

'The Survivor's Workshop' is designed for women who seek the skills to thrive in the great outdoors. This workshop offers a curriculum that includes navigation, detect edible plants, setting up your tent/camp, basic first aid. You will also learn how to respond to wildlife encounters and use survival tools effectively. Our wilderness experts will provide supportive, hands-on instruction, empowering each woman to become self-reliant in diverse environments. Whether facing the challenges of the road or the unpredictability of the woods, 'the Survivor's Workshop' equips you with the practical know-how for peace of mind and the freedom to explore. Start your adventure and join a community of like-minded women ready to conquer the wilderness with grace.

Wheels and Wisdom Workshop

Place for the natural go-getters. Learn about the ins and outs of your campervan, vehicle maintenance and how to deal with mechanincal issues.

Wheels and Wisdom Workshop

“Wheels and Wisdom” is a workshop designed to empower women by illuminating the technical aspects of campervan ownership. Over the course of this workshop, participants will learn essential skills such as vehicle maintenance, troubleshooting common mechanical issues, and understanding the electrical systems that keep their van running smoothly. Additionally, the workshop will cover practical tips for safe driving, efficient packing, and maximizing the living space within their campervan. Our expert instructors, with years of experience on the road, will provide personalized guidance, ensuring that every attendee leaves with the confidence to handle the technicalities of their vehicle. Whether you’re planning a weekend getaway or a cross-country adventure, “Wheels and Wisdom” equips you with the knowledge to embark on your journey with assurance and independence.

RoadWarrior Workshop

The workshop which calls for all women. Be ready to confidently start your journey on the road.

RoadWarrior Workshop

Are you a fearless adventurer, ready to hit the road with your camper van? Whether you’re chasing sunsets, exploring hidden trails, or simply seeking solitude, your safety is key. Join our 'RoadWarrior' workshop and equip yourself with essential self-defence skills to navigate the road confidently and protect yourself when needed. You will learn practical techniques to defend yourself against any potential threat. Our experienced instructors will guide you through manoeuvres to protect yourself from various angles—front, back, or even against multiple assailants. We will also focus on situational awareness. We’ll teach you how to assess your surroundings, spot potential risks, and make informed decisions to stay safe. Not to forget a basic first aid tutorial! Remember, adventure awaits, but safety is your compass. Don’t let fear hold you back
