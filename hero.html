Fuel Your Heart

The entertainment page to fuel your heart with.

One of the most valuable rewards from travelling is the stories you get to share with your friends, family and with the women on 'HerWay' of course. On this page women share their most beautiful, exciting and laugh-worthy travel stories.

You can even submit your own stories on the bottom of the page!

My Journey by Sophie

I was 20 and at university when I realized that my life wasn't meant to be for me to end up with a 9-5 job in an office. In my heart I knew I was meant to travel and see the world. I always had a dream of having my own van, so I quit university and started working full-time at my job to save up for my van renovation. No more waiting for retirement, no more counting the days until I finally get some days of the go someplace where I can feel the sun on my face.
