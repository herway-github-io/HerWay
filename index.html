About us

HerWay was founded in 2024 by four women who all had the same goal.

To create a platform to be your go-to-guide as a woman who start her travelling journey with a campervan. HerWay will help you to feel empowered, safe, educated and independent through tips and tricks, workshops, campsites and route recommendations and an overall look into the campervan lifestyle.

Our Vision

In a world where outdoor lifestyle is dominated by men, we believe that every woman deserves a sanctuary designed to nurture her growth, making her feel empowered and confident because only then can she discover her true self without any limiting boundaries.

Our Mission

At HerWay, our mission is to empower and support women as they embark on their journey of self-discovery. We strive to break down barriers, challenge stereotypes, and provide the tools and resources necessary for women to embrace the camper van lifestyle on their own terms. Through a commitment to empowerment, growth, independence, and safety, we aim to create a vibrant and inclusive space by women, for women, where females can thrive, explore, and create lasting memories on the open road.

Value #1

Empowerment

Providing women with the tools to embrace the van lifestyle on their own terms. Breaking free from gender stereotypes that may limit their  adventures.

Value #2

Growth

Fostering personal development space and learning environment within the camper van community.

Value #3

Independence

Encouraging self-sufficiency and resilience, for women to handle various situations they may encounter on the road with confidence.

Value #4

Safety

Support a sense of trust and reliability, ensuring women feel secure and protected throughout their journeys.
